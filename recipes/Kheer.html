Kheer (Rice Pudding)

this is indian dish you can see above and its name Kheer.

Kheer is a traditional Indian rice pudding made by slow-cooking rice in milk with sugar and flavored with cardamom, saffron, and nuts. It has a creamy and rich texture, making it a popular dessert for festive occasions.

Served warm or chilled, kheer is enjoyed across India with variations such as Seviyan (vermicelli) Kheer and Sabudana (tapioca) Kheer.

Ingredients

  1. 1 liter milk
  2. ¼ cup basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  3. ¼ cup sugar
  4. ¼ cup milk (warm)
  5. ½ tsp cardamom powder
  6. A few saffron strands (optional)
  7. 1 tbsp chopped almonds & pistachios
  8. 1 tbsp raisins

Steps

  1. Boil Milk: Heat milk in a heavy-bottomed pan and bring to a boil.
  2. Add Rice: Add soaked basmati rice and cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, until rice is soft and milk thickens.
  3. Sweeten & Flavor: Add sugar, cardamom powder, saffron, and dry fruits. Simmer for 5 more minutes.
  4. Serve: Garnish with more nuts and serve warm or chilled.
